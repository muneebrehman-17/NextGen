Your Onboarding Roadmap

To ensure a secure and swift setup, we'll need the following information from you.

1. Business Information

  • Legal Company Name: As registered with government agencies.
  • US Business Address: A physical address, no P.O. Boxes.
  • Employer Identification Number (EIN): Your federal tax ID.

2. Financial Setup

  • US Bank Account: For Walmart to deposit your earnings (Routing & Account #).
  • Dedicated Business Card: A separate credit/debit card for purchasing products.

3. Technical Setup

  • Dedicated Laptop: A basic laptop for our team to process orders securely.
  • Remote Access Software: We will help you install AnyDesk for secure remote access.

4. Primary Contact

  • Authorized Person: Name and email for verification and approvals.
