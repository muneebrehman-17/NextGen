Our Partnership Workflow

A transparent look into how we partner with your LLC to build and scale a profitable e-commerce asset on the Walmart marketplace.

Setting the Foundation

To ensure a smooth and compliant operation, we require a few key things from our partners.

LLC & Business Essentials

  • US-Based LLC: Your Limited Liability Company provides the legal structure for our partnership.
  • EIN Document: The Employer Identification Number is required for tax purposes and opening accounts.
  • Resale Certificate: Allows us to purchase products for resale without paying sales tax.

Store & Financials

  • Walmart Seller Account: The central hub for your e-commerce store on the marketplace.
  • Business Bank Account: Dedicated account for all store-related deposits and transactions.
  • Business Credit Card: Used for purchasing inventory and covering operational costs.

A Low-Risk Partnership

Our primary third-party model is designed to minimize your financial risk. We don't buy products until a customer places an order, meaning your capital isn't tied up in unsold inventory. This lean approach allows for greater flexibility and a safer investment in your e-commerce growth.
